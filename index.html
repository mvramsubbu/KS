THE COMPANY

The Group Parent Company named Shah Electronics is established since 1965 and is diversified into Manufacturing, Trading, Imports, Service and Specializes in providing all type of power source products such as AC to AC Transformers, AC to DC Power Supplies, DC to AC invertors & DC to DC convertors.

As part of diversification and expansion of group company Kwick Soft was established in 2005 and today it is the reliable supplier of quality products offering end to end Crime Scene Investigation related Products and Technologies, Kits and Modern Equipment to the Police Departments having all India presence. Kwick Soft Specializes in following:

> Supply of Equipment and Kits for Forensic Labs, Finger Print Labs & Cyber Crime Branches
> Indigenously developed first of kind 3D Interactive Crime Scene Training Simulator
> Automated Finger Print Identification System (AFIS) - PAPILLON
> Mobile Multi Spectral Imaging Systems - FORENSCOPE (Tablet & Finger Print Models) 
> Narcotics and Explosive detection products - XCAT
> Laser 3D Scanners to digitizing Crime Scene in 2D & 3D - FARO
> In house Research, Development and Service Centre facilities
> Specialized and High Tech Products-Surveillance System(CCTV), Mobile Interceptors etc.
> Extensive research and development of Portable Garbage disposal unit, portable water recycling process, Smart Fitness Bands for better lifestyle, Go Rich-Wealth Management advisory services, Development of Virtual Reality Games
