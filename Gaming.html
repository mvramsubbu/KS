Gif Booth

Gifbooth takes a quick burst of photos and turns them into a 2-3 second looping clip. The clip can then be shared to your phone, email and/or social media. It captures motion instead of still photos, making the end result something much more interesting than a regular image. It is fun and is a great way for promotion on social media and user attraction.

Social feeds wall

Social feeds wall application searches for posts from popular social networks like facebook, twitter, instagram. Then customizes the feeds for your own branding. It can be displayed on screen, projector on public place, gathering in live.
It is very useful for engaging your customers in events and marketing campaigns more interactive. This Incentivise your audience to promote your brand on social media. This increases brand insights by showing authentic user-generated content to build trust in your brand. 

Magic floor

It is a motion based floor graphics game where users can play games or see visual graphics as they move around the floor by creating a digitally engaging interactive gaming area for kids and adults. It engages a person in an unforgettable experience allowing them to immerse into the brand or just have fun. Its dynamic Interactive sound recognition makes great amusement for visitor. It is very useful for events, exhibitions, lobbies, malls, kids play area.

Piano Staircase

Piano staircase is an interactive playful musical stairway which is great for motivating people/employees to use stairs and sending a positive message that you care about them. It reproduces the sound of a real classical piano when someone steps on the stairs. It is very useful for events, exhibitions, lobbies and can be used to attract the crowd.

VR shooting

VR shooting game Defend the nation is a virtual reality game which allows you to experience the fun and challenge of shooting enemies from the front defense line of the city.

There are bomb flying around to be caught and blasted on enemies. It challenges you to shoot a perfect headshot in full momentum and and increase your accuracy in every shot.
