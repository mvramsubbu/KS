KWICK CRIMESIM - 3D Interactive Training Software Simulator

Kwick CrimeSim -is designed and developed in collaboration with Directorate of Forensic Science Services, Ministry of Home Affairs and Department of Science and Technology with inputs from world class police training academy from Andhra Pradesh and their experts from the top level were involved. The Objective to collaborate to develop the interactive software simulator was to bring to the awareness of the low and high level police personnel including the first time responders to the crime scene on the do's and dont's while visiting the crime scene.
